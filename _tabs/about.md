Cybersecurity plays a critical role in safeguarding communications and data in modern computer systems. A robust national strategy for cybersecurity that promotes foundational knowledge in this area is absolutely essential. PakCrypt NPO was founded to cultivate a critical mass of young talent with deep foundational knowledge in cryptography, mathematics, and computer science generally, to underpin trustworthy cybersecurity technologies in Pakistan.

### <span style="color: seagreen;"> **Our History** </span>
PakCrypt was initiated to promote research talent in cryptography and mathematics. The inaugural PakCrypt 2022, followed by PakCrypt 2023 and PakCrypt 2024, were resounding successes. But PakCrypt is more than just a competition or research community. It’s a platform that gives young minds opportunities to:

- connect with industry experts,

- receive job offers,

- secure funding for further research,

- and engage in collaborative projects.

<span style="color: blue;"> Your network is your net worth. — Porter Gale </span>


### <span style="color: seagreen;"> **Why PakCrypt.org was founded?** </span>
Our primary mission is to promote innovation. At the same time, we strive to foster a conducive environment for state-of-the-art research in Pakistan, ensuring that our industry remains at the forefront of advancements in this vital field.

![Closing](.{{site.baseurl}}/assets/images/about.jpg)

### <span style="color: seagreen;"> **PakCrypt Society** </span> 
PakCrypt Society's objectives are grouped under three key themes:

<span style="color: seagreen;"> **Empower Tomorrow's Minds:** </span>  PakCrypt partners with schools, universities, and industry to host workshops and seminars, and it organizes training programs and competitions to help students engage in advanced research in cybersecurity.

<span style="color: seagreen;"> **Research & Development:** </span>  The society fosters world-class cybersecurity R&D, builds core competencies, champions sustained funding, and promotes high-quality publications. 

<span style="color: seagreen;"> **Scientific Exchange:** </span>  We organize annual cybersecurity meetings, conferences, and competitions for members and the wider cryptography community. We facilitate international training and networking opportunities to help members and the wider cyber community advance their knowledge and skills.
